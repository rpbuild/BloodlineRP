Conduct & Standards

General Conduct

  • Treat all players and staff with respect.
  • Harassment, hate speech, or slurs are zero-tolerance.
  • No exploiting, cheating, or bug abuse.
  • English in main RP channels unless RP-appropriate.
  • Staff decisions are final (appeals available).

Roleplay Standards

  • Stay in character at all times in RP areas.
  • Use /me, /do, or server emotes properly.
  • No breaking immersion unnecessarily.
  • No OOC talk during active scenes.
  • Characters must act realistically.

Violations & Life Rules

RP Violations

  • RDM – Killing without valid RP initiation.
  • VDM – Using vehicles as weapons without RP.
  • Powergaming – Unrealistic actions or forcing RP.
  • Metagaming – Using OOC info IC.
  • FailRP – Ignoring realistic outcomes.

* These result in warnings, strikes, or harsher penalties.

Value of Life (VOL)

  • Your character values their life realistically.
  • Fear roleplay when threatened.
  • Outnumbered or outgunned scenarios require compliance.
  • Escaping danger should be logical and believable.

New Life Rule (NLR)

  • After being downed or respawned, you forget the event.
  • No returning to the same scene.
  • No revenge or continuation without staff approval.

Criminal & Gang Rules

Criminal Roleplay

  • Crimes require RP buildup and intent.
  • No random shootouts or robberies.
  • Initiation must be clear and verbal.
  • Gang wars require staff approval.
  • Drive-bys are restricted / disallowed.
  • Perma-death may be enforced for serious outcomes.

Gangs & Cartels

  • Staff approval required to form a gang.
  • Member limits enforced.
  • Territories assigned by staff.
  • Alliances and wars must be RP-approved.
  • Rule violations can result in disbandment.

Government & Medical

Law Enforcement

  • Escalation before force.
  • No power abuse or tunnel vision.
  • Bodycams always active.
  • Corruption RP requires approval.
  • DOJ oversight applies.

EMS Rules

  • Neutral party — no favoritism.
  • No weapon usage.
  • RP medical treatment properly.
  • Cannot relay IC information unlawfully.

DOJ & Courts

  • Judges and lawyers must remain impartial.
  • Evidence must be obtained legally.
  • Court RP should be professional.

Business & Administration

Business Roleplay

  • Must serve an RP purpose.
  • No money laundering without approval.
  • Owners responsible for employees.
  • Abuse may result in revocation.

Perma-Death

  • Can be enforced by staff or story outcome.
  • Requires serious RP.
  • Character death must be respected.

Staff & Enforcement

  • Staff may intervene to protect RP quality.
  • Punishments scale by severity.
  • Appeals handled via tickets.
