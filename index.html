The Streets of San Andreas

A City Divided

Los Santos is a metropolis of extreme contrasts. From the glittering high-rises of Rockford Hills to the gang-infested cul-de-sacs of Davis, opportunity and death walk hand in hand. The Mayor's office promises crackdowns, but corruption bleeds through the very foundations of the city.

North of the city limits lies Blaine County—a sprawling, dust-choked expanse where outlaw motorcycle clubs, cartel runners, and rogue militias operate far from the prying eyes of the city police. In Bloodline RP, every alleyway has a story, and every territory has a price.

Districts of Interest

South Central LS

The beating heart of gang warfare. Davis, Strawberry, and Chamberlain Hills are controlled by ruthless street syndicates fighting over the booming narcotics trade.

Vinewood & Rockford

Where the elites play. High-end car dealerships, luxury clubs, and corporate espionage. The perfect place for a clean heist or a messy assassination.

Sandy Shores

The unforgiving desert. A haven for those running from the law. Meth labs, illegal street racing, and brutal biker wars dominate the local economy.

Server Rules & Penal Code

OOC Guidelines and In-Character Laws. Ignorance is not an excuse.

General Roleplay (OOC)

1. Value of Life (NVL)

You must value your character's life at all times. If you are outnumbered or have a weapon drawn on you while unarmed, you must comply. Do not act like a fearless action hero.

2. Metagaming & Stream Sniping

Using out-of-character (OOC) information in-character (IC) is strictly banned. Watching a streamer playing on the server to find their location or plans will result in an immediate permanent ban.

3. Breaking Character

You must stay in character at all times. If there is a rule break or a bug, play through the scenario and report it on Discord later. Never use terms like "My game crashed" or "Press E" in voice chat.

4. Crime & Combat Rules

RDM/VDM: Killing another player without proper roleplay initiation or using a vehicle as a weapon is prohibited. You must have a verbal exchange before shooting.
Safe Zones: Pillbox Medical and spawn locations. Committing violent crimes or taking hostages here is forbidden.
