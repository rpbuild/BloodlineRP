Rules & Law

Respect The Scene Or Face The Fallout

Bloodline is designed for serious, high-pressure roleplay. These rules protect immersion, pace, and the quality of every story happening around you.

Core Standards

The rules that keep the city alive

Value Of Life

If your character is clearly at gunpoint, boxed in, or overwhelmed, act like survival matters.

No Metagaming

Keep all out-of-character info out of the city. Stream-sniping and Discord leaks kill the entire point of RP.

No Random Violence

Violence needs context, escalation, and scenes that make sense for the characters involved.

General Roleplay Conduct
Stay in character, use believable reactions, and play through issues instead of turning every bug into an OOC argument mid-scene.
Crime & Combat Rules
Initiation matters. Hostage scenes, robberies, shootouts, and gang pressure all need clear RP before bullets start flying.
Safe Zones & Staff Intervention
Spawn zones, hospital intakes, and active staff handling are not the place for grief scenes, powergaming, or forced pressure.
Economy & Asset Use
Premium cars, MLOs, and businesses still live inside the city economy. Use them to deepen RP, not bypass it.
