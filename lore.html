The City

Los Santos Is Split By Power

Bloodline RP turns San Andreas into a living map of factions, money routes, and dangerous neighborhoods where the wrong move can light up the whole city.

A City Divided

Luxury in the hills, blood in the blocks, money in the shadows

From Rockford Hills penthouses to Sandy Shores compounds, Bloodline is designed to support every kind of story: cartel meetings, courtroom wins, nightclub politics, and violent takeover scenes.

  • Southside districts drive gang stories, narcotics, and raw turf conflict.
  • Downtown and Vinewood fuel business roleplay, luxury lifestyles, and public image wars.
  • Blaine County stays wild with ranch compounds, biker pressure, and rural criminal operations.
Districts

Where your story starts changes what you survive

South Central LS

Davis, Strawberry, and Chamberlain are built for gang pressure, drug movement, and retaliation scenes that escalate fast.

Vinewood & Rockford

Luxury businesses, social flexing, private security, and white-collar money laundering all thrive where the cameras always roll.

Sandy Shores

County life is rough, isolated, and perfect for outlaw crews, biker chapters, farm operations, and hidden stash lines.

Factions

Every major lane in the city has real people behind it

Law Enforcement

LSPD, BCSO, and state units pressure the streets with felony stops, raids, warrant sweeps, and long investigations.

Cartels & Syndicates

Shadow organizations control imports, narcotics, contract violence, and the kinds of meetings nobody survives twice.

EMS & Medical

Pillbox scenes, trauma calls, surgeries, and hospital politics anchor every major gunfight and city-wide disaster.

Business Moguls

Nightclubs, dealerships, lounges, mechanic chains, and security firms control legal cash and cover dirty money.

Street Tuners

The underground scene moves through chop shops, race circuits, and high-risk import culture that police love to target.

DOJ & Politics

Judges, lawyers, and city officials decide whether a criminal goes down for years or walks back into the same block smiling.

Economy

Money is hard to make and worth protecting

Bloodline thrives when money has weight. Cars, MLOs, businesses, and criminal resources all matter because every purchase changes how your character moves.

Progression

Your name means more than your starter pack

Crews rise by the scenes they create, the pressure they hold, and the people they can keep loyal when the whole city is watching.
